About Us

Phungela Online Solutions is an online solutions provider that aims to provide business owners from all walks of life an opportunity to take advantage of the global market of customers world-wide by representing their business online with our high quality digital marketing services.

Our founder and C.E.O.,a web design enthusiast who goes by the name of Ntsikelelo Mnqokoyi, as shown in the picture above, realised the importance of a sound digital marketing strategy for businesses after seeing first-hand how powerful this can be especially for business owners who want to sell their goods/services to the entire world.

The name Phungela is derived from our founder's favorite ancestral clan name from his family's lineage
